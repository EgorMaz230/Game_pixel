SPACE CHAOS

Take a trip around the galaxy from the group "SPACE CHAOS"! Get ready to go on a sloppy journey, your children will be treated to different characters and will go through a low smoky level. You will also experience unique meteorites falling from the sky. The characters will have unique skills: some will be able to slash high, while others will be able to quickly roll left and right. You also have the opportunity to earn various bonuses and collectibles that will help you get through the level. The skin will be filled with colorful cosmic locations, including planets, asteroid fields and supernovae. You will have to show the rightness and reaction in order to eliminate insecurity and reach the final point of recognition. Collect additional bonuses and achieve new records, with different characters and endless treasures to become a space voyager!!

GENRE:
action, adventure, space

Features:
cooperative and single mode

  • More than once you will have to dodge meteorites and find gold on new planets.
  • From the very beginning you will have access to 3 characters, but for money you can buy additional ones.
  • In this game you can play alone or with friends. Set records among friends and also beat your friends' records.
